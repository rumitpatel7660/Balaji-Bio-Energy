About Balaji Bio Energy

Leading the way in sustainable energy solutions with 8 years of excellence

Our Story

Balaji Bio Energy is a leading manufacturer of high-quality biomass briquettes based in Jamnagar, Gujarat. With 8 years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a verified supplier of sustainable energy solutions.

Our manufacturing facility is equipped with advanced technology and follows strict quality control measures to ensure the highest standards in our products. We specialize in the production of various biomass briquettes including rice husk briquettes, sawdust briquettes, and wood briquettes.

Located in the industrial hub of Jamnagar, our facility is strategically positioned to serve both domestic and international markets. We take pride in our commitment to environmental sustainability and renewable energy solutions.
